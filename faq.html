calendar_month Booking & Rates

How do I book a studio session?

expand_more

Booking can be done directly through our online portal by clicking the "Book Now" button in the navigation bar. You can view real-time availability for all our rooms (Studio A, B, and the Mastering Suite). A 50% deposit is required to secure your time slot.

What is your cancellation policy?

expand_more

We require at least 48 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling to receive a full refund of your deposit. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice will forfeit the deposit.

Are engineers included in the hourly rate?

expand_more

Standard studio booking includes one assistant engineer. Lead recording engineers, mixing engineers, or session musicians can be booked at an additional hourly rate depending on the professional's level of experience.

Can I bring my own engineer?

expand_more

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own engineer. However, a brief orientation with our staff engineer is required before the session starts to ensure they are familiar with our specific hardware routing and patch bays.

What sample rate and bit depth do you record at?

expand_more

We record at 24-bit / 48kHz as standard. For demanding projects such as film scoring or Dolby Atmos, we record at 32-bit float / 96kHz. All sessions are exported at your preferred format.

Can I connect remotely to a live session?

expand_more

Yes. We support Source Connect Standard and Sessionwire for low-latency remote direction. Our upload speed is 1Gbps fibre, ensuring crystal-clear communication and audio streaming.

tune Mixing & Mastering

How many revisions are included in a mix?

expand_more

Our standard mixing package includes three full revisions. Additional revisions can be purchased at an hourly rate. We recommend providing detailed mix notes upfront to minimise revision rounds.

Do you offer Dolby Atmos mastering?

expand_more

Yes, our mastering suite is fully Dolby Atmos certified. We deliver ADM BWF files ready for Apple Music, Tidal, and Amazon Music HD. Stereo and spatial masters are delivered simultaneously.

help General Inquiries

Is parking available at the studio?

expand_more

Yes, we have dedicated parking for up to 10 vehicles directly outside the studio. Additional public parking is available within a 2-minute walk at no extra cost.

Do you cater food and beverages?

expand_more

We have a fully stocked kitchen and lounge area available to all clients. For longer sessions (4+ hours) we can arrange catering from local restaurants upon request. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 24/7 to help you with your production needs. Send us a message and we'll get back to you within the hour.
